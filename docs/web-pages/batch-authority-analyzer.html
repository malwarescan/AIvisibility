FAQ

How many domains can I analyze at once?
The Batch Authority Analyzer can process up to 50 domains simultaneously, depending on your plan. This allows for comprehensive portfolio analysis and competitive benchmarking.
How long does batch analysis take?
Analysis time depends on the number of domains and analysis depth. Typically, 10 domains take 5-10 minutes, while 50 domains may take 20-30 minutes. Progress is tracked in real-time.
Can I save batch analysis results?
Yes! You can save analysis results for future reference, export them in multiple formats (PDF, CSV, JSON), and share them with team members or clients.
What export formats are available?
The tool supports PDF reports, CSV data exports, JSON structured data, and custom report templates for different use cases and audiences.
How do I prioritize optimization efforts?
Focus on domains with the lowest authority scores first, then prioritize based on business value, effort required, and potential impact on AI search visibility.
